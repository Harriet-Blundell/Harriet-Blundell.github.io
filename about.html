Why coding?

At the beginning, my knowledge of JavaScript was very basic because I did not have the opportunity to learn about using programming languages whilst in education.

A friend of mine advised that I use freecodecamp to learn a basic introduction into JS, and from there my interest in learning about JavaScript grew.

I decided after a month of self-teaching myself JavaScript, I wanted to delve into knowing more about it at a higher level. After researching about courses in coding,

I came across Northcoders and discovered they led a coding bootcamp in the north.

I applied to attend their taster sessions and after having gone, I realised learning to code was something I wanted to pursue.
